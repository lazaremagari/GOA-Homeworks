C-4 or Composition C-4 is a common variety of the plastic explosive family known as Composition C,
which uses RDX as its explosive agent. C-4 is composed of explosives, plastic binder, plasticizer to
make it malleable, and usually a marker or odorizing taggant chemical. C-4 has a texture similar to
modelling clay and can be molded into any desired shape. C-4 is relatively insensitive and can be
detonated only by the shock wave from a detonator or blasting cap.
